## Controls
- ### Movement
    * `W`: Move Forward
    * `S`: Move Backward
    * `A`: Strafe Left
    * `D`: Strafe Right
    * `Shift Left`: Sprint (hold and press any of `[W, A, S, D]` to move)
    * `Space`: Jump
    * `C`: Crouch
    * `R`: Reset Camera
- ### Viewing
    * `Q`: Look Left
    * `E`: Look Right
    * `Arrow Up`: Look Up
    * `Arrow Down`: Look Down
    * `Arrow Left`: Roll Left
    * `Arrow Right`: Roll Right
- ### Shading Modes
    * `1`: Flat
    * `2`: Gouraud
    * `3`: Wireframe
